Pop Smoke (Bashar Barakah Jackson)

Brooklyn drill, hip-hop, trap

Bashar Barakah Jackson, known professionally as Pop Smoke, was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter who pioneered the Brooklyn drill music scene.

ACHIVEMENTS

    2019 - Breakout Hit — “Welcome to the Party” (2019)

    2019-2020 - “Meet the Woo” & “Meet the Woo 2” (Mixtapes)

    2020 - Posthumous Debut Album — Shoot for the Stars, Aim for the Moon (2020)

    2021 - Billboard Music Award (2021): Top New Artist

    2021 - Grammy Nomination (2021): “Dior” – Best Rap Performance
